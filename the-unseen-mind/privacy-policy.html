Privacy Policy for The Unseen Mind

Effective date: June 12, 2026

The Unseen Mind is an educational and self-reflection app about psychology, the unconscious mind, journaling, lessons, audio, quizzes, and related learning tools. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

The app may store your course progress, journal entries, quiz progress, settings, and app preferences on your device so the experience can function properly.

If you purchase a subscription, payments and subscription status are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, and may also be managed through RevenueCat for entitlement validation. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We may receive basic technical information needed to operate, debug, improve, and secure the app, such as device type, app version, operating system version, purchase entitlement state, crash diagnostics, and anonymous usage events if analytics are enabled.

Journal and Reflection Content

Journal entries and private reflections are intended to remain private. Unless the app later offers an explicit backup, sync, export, or account feature that you choose to use, journal content is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app experience, remember progress, unlock paid features, process subscriptions, improve reliability, understand feature usage, prevent fraud, and comply with platform and legal requirements.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. Subscription management, cancellation, refunds, and billing are handled by the store where you purchased the subscription. You can manage subscriptions from your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for subscription entitlement management, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or support. These services process data according to their own privacy terms and only for the purposes required to provide and improve the app.

Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Purchase records may be retained by Apple, Google, RevenueCat, or other service providers as required for subscription validation, tax, fraud prevention, support, and legal compliance.

Your Choices

You may delete local app data by deleting the app from your device. You may manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. You may contact us to request help related to privacy or app data.

Children's Privacy

The Unseen Mind is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Health and Educational Disclaimer

The Unseen Mind provides educational and self-reflection content. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency or need professional support, contact a qualified professional or emergency services in your area.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
