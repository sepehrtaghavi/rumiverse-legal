Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Overview

RumiVerse ("we," "our," or "the app") is a poetry and meditation app that respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

Local Data Only. RumiVerse stores all user data (journal entries, bookmarks, preferences, and progress) locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not transmit, store, or have access to any of your personal data on our servers.

No Account Required. RumiVerse does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in.

3. Subscription Data

If you subscribe to RumiVerse Premium, your payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier — no personal information is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics

RumiVerse does not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage data. We do not use any third-party tracking services.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: Handles payments and subscription management
  • RevenueCat: Verifies subscription status (anonymous identifiers only)
  • Google Fonts: Loads typefaces (standard web request, no tracking)

6. Children's Privacy

RumiVerse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app. No server-side data exists to delete.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: sepehr@rumiverse.app
