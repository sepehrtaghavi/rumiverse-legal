Subscription Management

To manage, cancel, or modify your RumiVerse Premium subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find RumiVerse and make changes

Your subscription will auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You will not be charged during the free trial if you cancel before it ends.

Restore Purchases

If you've previously subscribed and need to restore your purchase (e.g., on a new device), open RumiVerse and tap the "Restore Purchases" option on the subscription page.

FAQ

Is my data stored online?

No. All your journal entries, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal data.

What happens if I delete the app?

Your locally stored data (journal entries, progress) will be removed. Your subscription status is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored when you reinstall.

Can I use RumiVerse on multiple devices?

Your Premium subscription works on any device signed in with the same Apple ID. However, journal entries and progress are stored locally and do not sync between devices.
